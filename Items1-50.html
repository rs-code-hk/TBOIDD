The Sad Onion

Very Rare - Wondrous Item

"Tears Up"

You may make an extra ranged attack using your bonus action, if you made an attack with the same weapon using your action.

Interactions

  • This stacks with other items that give you extra attacks

The Inner Eye

Very Rare - Wondrous Item

"Triple Shot"

The first time you make an attack on your turn, you may replace your attacks with 3 of the same weapon. For instance, you could use this with a longbow, to replace your attacks with 3 longbow attacks.

Interactions

  • You can also use this item with other items that give you ranged weapon attacks.

  • This affect stacks with 20/20 and Mutant spider, allowing you to add the amount of attacks together.

Spoon Bender

Very Rare - Wondrous Item

"Homing Shots"

As long as you can draw a line equal to or less than your range to your target, you may make the attack as if they had half instead of full cover. You may also ignore half cover.

Interactions

This affect also works with a line area of affect.

My Reflection

Very Rare - Wondrous Item

"Boomerang tears + DMG up + luck down"
